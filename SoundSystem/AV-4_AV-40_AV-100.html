AV-4 / AV-40 / AV-100 Volume Controllers

 

ATTENUATORS

 

 

100 V Line attenuators. Built-in security paging system that allows the messages go through at full power, no matter the position of the volume control. The 3 different power handling capacities, 6 W in the AV-4, 40 W in the AV-40 and 100 W in the AV-100, allow to cover any specific power requirement.

 

All the models include the embedding plastic box. The surface mounting boxes are available under request (C-417S for AV-4 and C-417S2 for the AV-40 and AV-100).
